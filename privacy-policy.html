Privacy Policy

Introduction

We value your privacy and strive to protect your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information, as well as your rights regarding your personal data.

We understand the importance of privacy for our clients, especially when it comes to financial services such as cryptocurrencies, trading, and investments. We are committed to protecting your information and ensuring transparency in how we handle it.

1. Information Collection

We may collect various types of information about you when you use our services:

  • Personal Information: name, contact information (email, phone number), address, banking information.
  • Financial Information: data about your investments, trading income, and other financial activities.
  • Technical Information: IP address, device and browser data, information about your visits to our website and use of our online services.
  • Cryptocurrency Information: data about your transactions, portfolio, and other cryptocurrency operations.

2. Use of Information

We use the collected information for various purposes:

  • Service Provision: processing your requests, executing financial transactions, providing investment advice and recommendations.
  • Improvement of Our Services: analyzing your preferences and behavior to enhance the quality and range of services offered.
  • Marketing and Communications: informing you about new products, services, special offers, and news related to cryptocurrencies, trading, and investments.
  • Security: ensuring the security of your data and preventing fraud and other illegal activities.

3. Disclosure to Third Parties

We may disclose your information to third parties in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: we may engage third-party companies to perform certain functions, such as payment processing, data analysis, and marketing research. These companies are required to maintain the confidentiality of your data and use it only to fulfill their obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: we may disclose your information to governmental authorities or other organizations when required by law or in response to lawful requests.
  • Mergers and Acquisitions: in the event of reorganization, merger, or sale of our assets, we may transfer your information to the new owners of the company.

4. Data Protection

We take all necessary measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Technical Measures: use of encryption, access control systems, and other modern technologies to protect data.
  • Organizational Measures: restricting access to data only to those employees who need it to perform their duties.
  • Procedural Measures: regular security checks and audits, as well as employee training on data protection issues.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: you can request a copy of your personal data that we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: you can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information about you.
  • Right to Erasure: you can request deletion of your data if it is no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected, or if you have withdrawn your consent for its processing.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: you can request restriction of processing of your data in certain cases, for example, if you dispute the accuracy of the data.
  • Right to Data Portability: you can receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format and transfer it to another data controller.
  • Right to Object: you can object to processing of your data in certain cases, such as for marketing purposes.

6. Children's Policy

Our services are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have received personal information from a child without parental consent verification, we will delete this information as soon as possible.

7. Cookies and Other Technologies

We use cookies and other technologies to collect information about your use of our website and improve its functionality. Cookies allow us to recognize your browser and store certain information. You can configure your browser to reject cookies or notify you about their use, but this may affect the functionality of some features of our website.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Policy on our website and indicating the date of the last update. We recommend that you periodically review this page to stay informed about changes.
